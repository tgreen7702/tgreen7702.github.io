How to interpret interactive terms?

### Interaction term should be included if it makes sense conceptually (meaning that it makes sense that the effect of one independent variable on DV is likely dependent on the effect of another independnt variable). Interpret the interaction term if significant; if insignificant, we can remove it from the model. Only interpret the interaction term, not the independent variables used to create it. The significant interaction term itself is difficult to interpret becasue it implies uncertainty about the relative importance of main effects. In other words, the relative importance of individual predictors is difficult to understand if interaction effects are significant.

Read: Brambor, T., Clark, W.R. and Golder, M., 2006. Understanding interaction models: Improving empirical analyses. Political analysis, 14(1), pp.63-82.
